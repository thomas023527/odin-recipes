Chicken Courgette

Boneless chicken with fresh zucchini in a tangy tomatoey sauce served over pasta.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon & 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 12 ounces tomato
  • 8 ounces zucchini
  • 2 ounces green bell pepper
  • 2 ounces onion/li>
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup French salad dressing
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon tarragon
  • 8 ounces pasta
  • 4 side salads
  1. Cut chicken into cubes and brown in oil over medium heat.
  2. Fill a large saucepan with water and heat on high.
  3. Dice tomato, zucchini, green pepper onion and garlic. Add to chicken and stir.
  4. Add French salad dressing, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, pepper and tarragon. Stir to blend and reduce heat to low.
  5. When water in large saucepan reaches boiling, add pasta and continue cooking over high heat for about 8 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  6. Check pasta for doneness by biting through a piece. If no white center appears, pasta is done. Drain and rinse.
  7. our chicken and zucchini sauce over pasta and serve with salad.
